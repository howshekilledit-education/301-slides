TouchDesigner Operators

  • TOPS: Texture Operators (image processing)
  • CHOPS: Channel Operators (audio and control signals)
  • SOPS: Surface Operators (3D geometry)
  • COMPS: Components (containers for networks)
  • MATS: Materials (shaders and surface properties)
  • DATS: Data Operators (tables and text data)

Helpful Shortcuts

  • Tab: Open operator menu
  • Spacebar: Play/stop timeline
  • F1: Enter Performance Mode
  • Esc: Exit Performance Mode

Todays Demo CHOPs

  • Audio Device In CHOP: Capture audio input from microphone
  • Audio Spectrum CHOP: Analyze audio frequencies
  • MouseIn CHOP: Track mouse position
  • Math CHOP: Perform mathematical operations on channels
  • Null CHOP: Pass channels through without modification (lets you switch things out later)

Other Operators

  • Box SOP: Create a 3D box geometry
  • Geo COMP: Container for 3D geometry
  • Camera COMP: Define the viewpoint for rendering
  • Light COMP: Add lighting to the 3D scene
  • Render TOP: Render the 3D scene to a 2D image
  • Transform TOP: Apply transformations to images, including background color

Project 1 Proposals

Project 1 Requirements

  • Use input from your computer’s microphone and/or mouse to generate or manipulate visual content in real-time.
  • The project should run in TouchDesigner and be presented in Performance Mode.
  • You can activate it as a performance, or have it user-activated (e.g. by mouse movement or sound input).
  • The project should have a strong conceptual basis and/or be a well-reseearched exploration of a specific aesthetic or visual style

Workshop your proposals

  1. Get back into your performance groups.
  2. Each person should take 2-3 minutes to read their proposal for Project 1. Feel free to add additional details beyond what you wrote in your forum post.
  3. After each pitch, the rest of the group should provide feedback and suggestions on
    • Feasibility
    • Process of making
    • Conceptual basis
    • Aesthetic or visual style
  4. Feedback should be respectful, but honest. Consider a compliment sandwich: positive feedback, constructive criticism, positive feedback.
  5. Write down feedback as you will need to revise your proposal based on the feedback you receive.
